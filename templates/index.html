What Is This Project About?

Mallampati photo booth is a non-commercial medical community project, consisting in harversting data about airway evaluation before anesthesia, and processing it through machine learning algorithms. Airway evaluation is a highly important topic in medicine, as a correct evaluation will allow caregivers to avoid life-threatening unexpected situations, where the patient cannot be ventilated when under anesthesia. Literature states a set of very precise criteria and measures for airway evaluation, and routine Mallampati scoring is part of those. By contributing to this project, you will help the community take a small step towards automated airway evaluation for a faster and precise airway evaluation before anesthesia, that will one day save lives! How May I Contribute?

You will find below a photo booth application that takes snapshots, and stores them into a database. You can contribute by taking a snapshot of your oral cavity, that will be used to determine your Mallampati score (detailed instructions below). No personal data will be required except this picture, and the pictures will not display your full face nor any easily recognizable facial features, thus guaranteeing a completely anonymous process. If you accidentally take a unsatisfactory snapshot, you will have the opportunity to discard it immediately.

Note: you may also contribute by uploading a picture from your device.

Are You Going To Sell My Data?

No, this project is totally free, non-commercial, and not-for-profit. We will never make use of your data out of this project, and will never give access to it to another organization. Your data will serve only medical research purposes.
